    • Surya Namaskar

    Surya Namaskar is my favourite yoga exercise. It makes the entire body flexible and prepares it for asanas, it is an sequence of twelve postures completed as one continuous cycle.Surya namaskar reduces stiffness, rejuvenates the body and invigorates the mind. It trimming the waist and makes the joints and spine supple. It is ideally done in the morning facing the east by offering homage to the sun through different postures and salutations.
    The twelve stages that constitute one suryanamaskar cycle are:-

  • 1. Namaskar Mudra

  • 2. Back Bending chakrasana

  • 3. Padahastasana

  • 4. Ashwa sanchalanasana

  • 5. Santolanasana

  • 6. Shashtanga mudra

  • 7. Sarpasana

  • 8. Parvatasana

  • 9. Ashwa Sanchalanasana

  • 10. Padadhastasana

  • 11. Back bending chakrasana

  • 12. Namaskar Mudra.
