Puran Poli
Ingredients

    Puran Poli usually prepased in Maharshtra for many festivals,its my favorate dish we always eat Puran poli on festival days. Puran Poli is a flat bread stuffed with a sweet lentil filling made from skinned spilt Bengal gram/chana dal & jaggery .In Marathi language the sweet filling is called as puran and the bread is called as poli

  • 1)I cup powdered jiggery (gur or gud )

  • 2)I cup chana dal (skinned spilt bengal gram)

  • 3)3cups water for pressure cooking the chana dal

  • 4)2teaspoon ghee

  • 5)I teaspoon saunth (dry giger powder )

  • 6)1/2 tespoon cardamom powder (choti elaichi)

  • 7)1/4 tespoon nutmeg powder (jaiphal powder)


    • For the poli – Outer Covering:-

  • 1)1.5 Cups whole wheat flour (attaa) +1/2 cup all purpose flour (maida) OR you can also use 2 cups whole wheat flour instead of maida.

  • 2)4 tablespoon ghee (mostly try to use cow ghee)

  • 3)salt as required

  • 4)1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder (haldi- optional- to give a faint yellow colour to the poli)

  • 5)Water as required to knead the dough

  • 6)Ghee as required for frying the puran poli.

Preparation
  • How to prepare Recipe:-

  • 1)Rinse the chana dal first very well in water

  • 2)In a pressure cooker cook the chana dal for 6to7 whistles.Once the pressure settles down on its own, strain the cooked dal. The dal has to be strained well.

  • 3)keep the stock aside ,this stock cam be used for making katkachi amti or you can just add it to your veggie dishes or use for gravy.

  • 4)heat ghee in a pan and add the dry ginger powder,nutmeg powder,cardamom powder and sauté for a few seconds.

  • 5)add the the chana dal and jiggery.stir and let this Puran mixture becomes dry.In winter you can add little bit keshar in Puran.

  • 6)keep on stirring ,once the puran stuffing become dry and thick ,switch off the flame.

  • 7)let it cool and then mash the puran mixture ,you can also use your mixer to mash the puran mixture very well ,keep aside.


  • Making the puran poli:-

  • 1) Take a medium size ball from the dough, roll it 2-3 inchase in circumference on a dusted rolling board.

  • 2) Place a portion of puran mixture in the center of the rolled dough

  • 3) Bring the edges together towards the center ,join all the edges and pinch them.

  • 4) Sprinkle some flour and start rolling the dough

  • 5) Make a medium or large circle (poli)

  • 6) On a heated tawa or griddle, spread some ghee ,place the rolled poli dough circle on the tawa.

  • 7) When one side gets browned ,turn over and cook the other side till you see some brown spots.

  • 8) Once the second side gets browned , then turn over and apply ghee. If everything is done properly then puran poli will puff up

  • 9) You can serve Puran poli warm or at room temperature with milk,ghee,Katachi aamti or aamras (mango Juice).


Nutritional benefits
  • 1. Whole wheet flour is rich in complex carbs, fibre ,gives B complex vitamins, certain minerals

  • 2. Dal(Bengal gram) used in it loaded with good amount of protein ,jaggery loaded with iron , calcium ,potassium, phosphorus.

  • 3. It helps in releasing energy slowly.cardamom powder , saffron ,nutmeg powder in puran poli helps in boosting digestion.


Ukdiche Modak
Ingredients
  • 1)Rice flour ½ Cups

  • 2)salt a pinch

  • 3)Ghee I teaspoon for greasing


  • For stuffing:-


  • 1)Fresh coconut grated 1½ cup

  • 2)Jaggery (gur) grated 1 cup

  • 3)poppy seeds (khuskhus) roasted 1 tablespoon

  • 4)Green Cardamom powder a pinch

  • 5)Nutmeg Powder a Pinch


Preparation
  • 1)Heat one and one fourth cups of water with Pinch salt (same people Not add salt) and 1 teaspoon ghee in a deep non stick pan

  • 2)Bring to a boli, reduce heat, and add the rice flour in a steady flow ,stirring continuously to prevent lumps from forming .cover the pan with deep lid and pour some water into the lid.cook on low heat for three minutes.

  • 3)Remove the lid sprinkle some cold water on the rice flour and cover again with the lid and cook for another three Minutes , repeat this process twice, take the pan off the heat and keep it covered for two Minutes

  • 4)Transfer the Mixture to a large plate , grease the palms of your hands with ghee and knead the dough till Completely smooth and Pliable .the dough should not stick to your palms .Rest the dough with a damp cloth

  • 5)For the stuffing ,combine the greated coconut and jaggery in non stick pan and cook on medium heat for one or two minutes till light golden brown.make sure that you do not overcook the mixture ,add the roasted poppy seeds ,cardamom powder and nutmeg powder.

  • 6)Divide the dough into twelve equal portions and shape them in to balls . Grease the palms with ghee and spread each ball to form a three inch bowl .Press the edges of the bowls to reduce the thickness

  • 7)Place a portion of the stuffing in the centre ,pleat the edges of the dough and gather them together to form a bundle, pinch to seal the edges at the top .even you can use ready made molds of modak to make it easily,molds are available in the market.

  • 8)Heat sufficient water in a steamer,place the modak on a perforated plate in the steamer and steam for ten to twelve minutes.on Perforated plate place very thin catton cloth them put modaks on that, modak will not stick on plate.

  • 9)Serve hot modak with pure ghee... yummy!


Nutritional benefits
  • 1) Modak rich in calcium ,proteins ,fiber ,also contain some amount of sodium, iron, vitamin A

  • 2) Coconut stuffing rich in calaries, carrbohydrates and saturated fats .
